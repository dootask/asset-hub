# Asset Hub – IT Asset & Consumable Management for DooTask

Asset Hub is an IT asset and consumable lifecycle management plugin for DooTask. It helps you manage asset registers, approval flows, inventory checks, and analytical reports directly inside the same collaboration platform.

## ✨ Highlights

- **Full asset lifecycle** – Covers purchase, inbound, assignment, borrow / return, distribution, repair, scrap, recycle, lost and more, with complete operation history.
- **Consumable control** – Maintains consumable ledgers, inbound/outbound records, minimum stock alerts, inventory checks, and warning reports.
- **Visual dashboard** – Home dashboard with KPIs such as asset scale, distribution, value trend, plus quick access to approvals and common actions.
- **Built‑in approval** – Embedded approvals around asset operations with states like draft, pending, approved, rejected, and cancelled.
- **Multi‑dimensional reports** – Analyze assets and consumables by company, department, category, status, etc., with export and audit support.
- **Deep DooTask integration** – Reuses DooTask users, permissions, and theme. No extra login; works together with tasks / to‑dos.

## 🚀 What can you do with Asset Hub?

- Build a centralized asset and consumable ledger and get rid of scattered spreadsheets.
- Standardize request, borrow/return, and scrap processes to reduce loss and clarify responsibility.
- Improve transparency and traceability of asset management through approvals and operation logs.
- Run regular inventories to understand real stock and idle assets.
- Gain insights into asset input–output, departmental usage, and lifecycle cost through reports.

## 👥 Typical roles

- **System / Asset Administrator**
  - Maintain companies and organization structure.
  - Configure asset categories, fields, statuses, and operation types.
  - Handle purchase, inbound, transfer, scrap, and other operations.
  - Manage inventory tasks and data exports.

- **Approver (manager / finance / asset owner, etc.)**
  - Approve purchase, inbound, assignment, borrow / return, and other requests.
  - View pending approvals and history in the approval center.

- **Employee / Requester**
  - Submit requests for asset assignment, borrow / return, etc.
  - View assets under their responsibility and related operation records.

The actual features each user can access depend on permissions configured by the workspace administrator in DooTask.

## 📥 How to open Asset Hub in DooTask

1. Ask your workspace administrator to install and enable the **Asset Hub** plugin from the DooTask app marketplace or admin console.
2. In DooTask, open the left‑side menu or the app center and locate **Asset Hub**.
3. Click to enter the Asset Hub home page. Your current login session, language, and theme will be reused automatically; no extra login is required.

If you see a “no permission” message, please contact your workspace administrator to grant you the necessary Asset Hub access and roles.

## 🔐 Permissions & data security

- Asset Hub is built on top of DooTask accounts and permission system; no separate login is needed.
- Menus and operations differ by role and by how the administrator has configured access.
- All critical actions are recorded as asset operations and approvals for auditability.

## ❓ FAQ

- **Q: I see a “no permission” message. What should I do?**  
  A: Contact your workspace administrator to grant you access to the Asset Hub plugin and the appropriate roles (such as asset admin, approver, or standard user).

- **Q: Will Asset Hub data affect other DooTask modules?**  
  A: Asset Hub primarily manages asset and consumable data, and integrates with DooTask via approvals and to‑dos. Whether it is linked with other modules (such as tasks or workflows) depends on your administrator’s configuration.

- **Q: Where can I learn how to use Asset Hub in detail?**  
  A: From the Asset Hub home page, go to the **Help Center** for official examples and recommended flows. You can also align Asset Hub with your internal asset policies and processes.
